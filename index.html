Hi, I'm Angie Fong.

I'm a master student studying data science and working part-time as a data analyst with previous education and some experience in marketing analytics.

In my previous role as a marketing operation intern at Tmall Global of Alibaba Group, I facilitated the delivery of new advertising product, and the launching of multiple mega events like Double 11. I appreciated the agile and fast-paced environment, but I have also recognized the power of data in the process which motivated the pursuit of my Master of Science in Information Studies degree.

The technical knowledge I gained in school led me to my current role as a marketing analyst intern where I'm mainly responsible for analyzing the marketing and sales performance across the funnel where I constantly work with Python, SQL, Tableau and Excel. I have been recognized by my supervisor for my ability to present data in easily digestible ways and ensure data integrity in the process.

I now look forward to landing my first full-time data science job in a data-mature company where the abundance of valuable insights and data-driven decision making is enabled.
